Regulatory Support for Multiplex Housing
Simplifying the path of duplexes, triplexes and fourplexes
Many municipalities are updating zoning rules to encourage the development of small-scale multi-unit housing. These changes make it easier to build duplexes, triplexes and fourplexes without complex rezoning processes. By allowing more units on typical residential lots and reducing parking requirements, these policies aim to increase housing supply, promote affordability and support vibrant, walkable neighborhoods. Homeowners now have more opportunities to convert or rebuild properties into multiplexes, helping meet growing housing demand while maintaining community character.
